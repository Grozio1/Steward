Steward

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026  ·  Wortman IP Holdings LLC

Steward is a manual-entry financial life companion. You type in your numbers. We do not connect to your bank, import your transactions, or collect data from anywhere other than what you type. This policy explains what happens to that information.

What Steward Collects

Steward collects only what you enter directly into the app:

  • Income, expenses, debts, and savings figures you provide during onboarding and updates
  • Life context you choose to share (household size, goals, life stage)
  • Financial decisions and notes you record over time

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials. We do not require an account to use the app.

Where Your Data Lives

Everything you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. There is no Steward server. Your financial data does not leave your phone except as described below under AI Features.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

AI Features

Steward uses Anthropic's AI to generate your financial plan, observations, and guidance. When you request AI-powered output, the financial information relevant to that request is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. That text is used solely to produce your result and is not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API call.

Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

No AI output is shared with third parties. No financial data is used to train AI models.

Subscriptions

Steward offers optional paid subscriptions (Pro and Family tiers) managed entirely through Apple's App Store. All billing, payment processing, and subscription management is handled by Apple. Wortman IP Holdings LLC does not receive, store, or have access to your payment information.

Apple's privacy policy governs App Store transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.

No Advertising. No Data Sales.

Steward contains no advertising. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties for commercial purposes. We make money from subscriptions only.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

Steward does not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs. We do not track usage behavior, session length, screen views, or any similar metrics.

Children's Privacy

Steward is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Steward after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of those changes.
